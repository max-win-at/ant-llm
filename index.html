How it works

  • Ants are persistent agents stored in LocalStorage.
  • Pheromone trails are maintained in IndexedDB.
  • Food is fetched from public APIs and stored as browser Cookies (with natural expiry).
  • Dangers come from HTTP error codes (429, 500) simulated via httpstat.us.
  • The colony reproduces when enough food is gathered, and ants die from energy loss or predators.
